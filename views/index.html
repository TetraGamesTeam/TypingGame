What is TypeAce?

TypeAce is an online service designed to help people learn blind typing, which is the ability to type without looking at the keyboard. The service offers a range of typing exercises and games that are designed to improve typing speed and accuracy, as well as a variety of lessons that cover the basics of touch typing. TypeAce also provides real-time feedback on typing performance, including metrics like words per minute and accuracy rate, which can help users track their progress over time. With its user-friendly interface and engaging content, TypeAce is a great resource for anyone looking to improve their typing skills, whether for personal or professional reasons.
